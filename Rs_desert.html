Anubis

Anubis was the ruler of an ancient city. He ruled his land with strength and prestige. His only goal was conquest. Ideologies of others did not concern him. The gods held powers which he sought out to get. They're followers were mere vessels of power he could tap from. The only god he acknowledged was Zaros. The ideology of power was something he could relate to. Yet he would not pray to any god, even Zaros. This did not shun Anubis from the god, but rather gave him his blessings. Zaros has chosen another vessel to carry his power.
